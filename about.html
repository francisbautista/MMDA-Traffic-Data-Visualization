data-driven dashboard on H-M-L traffic volume for Metro Manila

Donut Chart

[doh-nuh t, -nuht]

This donut chart visualizes the frequency percentages for high, medium, and low traffic volume recordings for the specific station at a specific time and day. The red slice represents heavy traffic, yellow for medium, and green for light. NB indicates the northbound line, and SB indicates the southbound line.


The MMDA Data 2-1-0 values were used for the H-M-L value analysis. -1 values were disregarded from the data set and taken away form the total percentage tally.
